AE-Print · Layer-by-Layer Concept

Existing additive manufacturing technology forces a choice: micro-precision or industrial scale. AE-Print eliminates that trade-off — delivering resolution previously only achievable in laboratory conditions, at factory throughput.

Eight material streams processed in a single build cycle. Metals, ceramics, high-performance polymers and composites — combined at the micro-scale without post-processing or assembly.

Applications

Where conventional manufacturing reaches its limits

Medical implants with integrated drug-delivery channels. Aerospace components combining structural metal with thermal ceramic. Custom photonic circuits. Advanced sensor housings with embedded electronics.

These components currently require multiple specialist vendors, months of lead time, and extensive post-processing. AE-Print produces them in a single build cycle.
